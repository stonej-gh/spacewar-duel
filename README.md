# Spacewar: Orbital Duel

**Battle at the singularity.** A fast one-on-one space duel fought in the gravity
well of a black hole — slingshot around the singularity, bend your shots through
its pull, and out-fly an AI that gets sharper the better you do.

## The idea

There's no flat space here. A black hole sits at the center of the screen and
pulls on **everything** — you, your opponent, and every shot fired. Good pilots
don't fly in straight lines: they ride orbits, use the hole's gravity to swing
around and line up shots, and treat the singularity as both the deadliest hazard
and their best weapon. Learn to think in orbits and you'll win.

## Controls

- **Steer — the ATTITUDE dial.** Drag your finger around the circular **ATTITUDE**
  wheel (the two-ring dial). Your ship rotates by however far you sweep *around*
  the dial — like turning a knob. How far your finger is from the center doesn't
  matter; the **angle** you travel does.
- **Fire — tap anywhere.** A tap anywhere on the screen fires your laser —
  including right on the dial. Tap-tap-tap to let loose.
- **Thrust — hold.** Hold the **THRUST** bar to burn your engine. By default you
  can also just **press and hold anywhere** to thrust (see Settings).
- **Mind the hole.** Your shots curve in the black hole's gravity, and so do you.
  Fly too close and you're gone. It takes several hits to down a ship, so keep
  the pressure on.

## Make the controls yours

Tap the **gear** (next to the pause button) during a game to enter **layout mode**:

- **Drag** the THRUST bar and the ATTITUDE dial wherever your thumbs land — left
  side, right side, anywhere.
- **Tap the THRUST bar** to flip it between **vertical** and **horizontal**.
- Your layout is **saved** and sticks across games and launches. Tap **Done** when
  it feels right.

## Settings

From the menu **gear**:

- **Start level** — how tough the AI begins.
- **Thrust pad** — show or hide the on-screen THRUST bar.
- **Hold to thrust** — press-and-hold anywhere to burn, and how long the press
  must be.
- **SFX volume.**

## Goal

Knock out your opponent five times to win a match. Win matches to climb a
difficulty ladder — the AI keeps getting smarter — and chase a high score. Then
beat it.
